# UltimateView HD Progressive Lenses

## What Is UltimateView HD?

UltimateView HD is 39DollarGlasses' premium progressive lens line. These are digitally surfaced, free-form progressive lenses — a significant step up from conventional progressives in optical quality and precision.

Free-form technology allows the lens to be surfaced to a much finer degree of precision than conventional grinding methods. The result is a lens that more accurately maps your specific prescription across the entire lens surface.

## Key Benefits

- **Wider viewing zones** — the distance, intermediate, and reading areas of the lens are wider, reducing the peripheral softness common in standard progressives.
- **Sharper clarity** — prescription accuracy is improved across more of the lens.
- **Better adaptation** — many wearers find UltimateView HD easier to adjust to, particularly if they have struggled with standard progressives in the past.
- **Suited to complex prescriptions** — the free-form design handles higher powers, significant astigmatism, and larger add powers more effectively than conventional progressives.

## Who Should Consider UltimateView HD?

UltimateView HD is a strong choice for:

- First-time progressive wearers who want the best chance of a smooth adaptation
- People with higher prescriptions or significant astigmatism
- People who have had difficulty adapting to standard progressives in the past
- Anyone who spends extended time at a computer or switching frequently between near and far tasks
- Anyone who wants the best optical quality available from 39DollarGlasses

## Frame Compatibility

UltimateView HD lenses require adequate vertical lens height — at least 28mm, with 30mm or more preferred. The wider viewing zones benefit most from frames with a larger lens area. See the progressives frame guidance in **progressives.md**.

## Coating Recommendation

Anti-reflective coating is highly recommended with UltimateView HD lenses. Because free-form lenses are often made in high-index materials, AR coating reduces the additional internal reflectivity common in denser lens materials and improves the overall visual experience.
