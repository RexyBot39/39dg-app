# Disclaimers and Limitations

## General Advisory Disclaimer

The 39DollarGlasses Lens & Frame Advisor provides general educational information about lens types, coatings, specialty lens products, and frame selection. It is intended to help you understand your options and explore products — not to replace the advice of an eye care professional.

The advisor does not have access to your prescription, your order history, your account, or any personal information. All guidance provided is general in nature.

## Prescription Disclaimer

The advisor cannot evaluate, validate, or interpret your specific prescription. A valid prescription from a licensed eye care professional is required to order prescription eyewear. If you have questions about your prescription — whether a specific power is valid, what your measurements mean, or whether a particular lens type is appropriate for your prescription — please consult your eye care professional.

The advisor may offer general guidance such as "high-index lenses are often recommended for prescriptions above ±4.00" but this is educational context, not a prescription recommendation.

## Medical Disclaimer

No content provided by this advisor constitutes medical advice. Products such as Neurolux, OcuSleep, Blue495, and other specialty lenses are consumer eyewear products, not medical devices or treatments. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition.

If you experience eye discomfort, pain, sudden vision changes, light sensitivity as part of a medical condition, sleep disorders, migraines, or other health concerns, please consult a qualified eye care professional or physician.

## Product Availability Disclaimer

Product availability, pricing, and options may change. The advisor recommends products based on current catalog data, but inventory and offerings are subject to change. For the most current availability and pricing, refer to the product page on the 39DollarGlasses website.

## Frame Compatibility Disclaimer

Guidance about frames for progressive lenses or strong prescriptions is general educational information. The suitability of a specific frame for a specific prescription depends on many factors including exact prescription values, pupillary distance, frame dimensions, and lens design. 39DollarGlasses does not guarantee that any frame will produce a specific optical outcome for a specific prescription. If you have concerns, our support team and your eye care professional can help.

## Support Handoff

For questions about your specific order, prescription, account, refunds, remakes, or any other account-level matter, the advisor will direct you to the 39DollarGlasses customer support team. The advisor does not have access to these systems and cannot assist with account-specific issues.
