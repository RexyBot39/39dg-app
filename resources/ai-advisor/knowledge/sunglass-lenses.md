# Sunglass Lenses

## Prescription Sunglasses

## Standard Sunglass Tints

Sunglass lenses are available in several tint options:

- **Gray** — neutral tint that reduces brightness without shifting colors; the most popular sunglass choice for general use
- **Brown/amber** — warms color perception and can enhance contrast in partially cloudy or variable light conditions
- **Green** — provides good color balance with natural color perception and contrast
- **Other tints** — fashion colors may be available; confirm options at checkout

Tint darkness levels are typically described as 75–80% light reduction for a standard sunglass tint.

## Polarized Lenses

Polarized lenses include a filter that blocks horizontally polarized light — the type of glare that reflects off flat surfaces like water, wet roads, and snow. Benefits include:

- Significantly reduced glare in bright outdoor conditions
- Improved visual comfort and clarity when near water or driving
- Better contrast and detail in high-glare environments

Polarized lenses are especially popular for driving, fishing, boating, skiing, and other outdoor activities where surface glare is a factor.

**Note on polarized and screens**: Polarized lenses can make some LCD screens appear dim or distorted depending on screen orientation. This is a physical property of the polarizing filter and not a defect in the lens. If you use a phone, GPS, or dashboard screen frequently while wearing sunglasses, keep this in mind.

## UV Protection

All sunglass lenses from 39DollarGlasses include UV protection. UV protection blocks ultraviolet light from reaching your eyes and is a standard feature in prescription and non-prescription sunglass lenses.

A darker tint does not automatically mean more UV protection. Our lenses include UV protection regardless of tint depth.

## Lens Material Options for Sunglasses

Prescription sunglass lenses can be made in polycarbonate or high-index materials for stronger prescriptions. High-index sunglass lenses allow thinner, lighter lenses in wrap-style or larger sunglass frames.
