# Lumeo Lenses

## What Are Lumeo Lenses?

Lumeo lenses are specialty prescription lenses from 39DollarGlasses designed for use in smart glasses and wearable eyewear frames. As smart glasses — frames with embedded cameras, speakers, microphones, and other technology — become more common, Lumeo provides a way to get prescription lenses made for these devices at an accessible price.

## What Smart Glasses Are Lumeo Lenses For?

Lumeo is designed to support compatible smart glasses frames. The specific frames and models supported may vary. If you have a specific smart glasses frame and are wondering whether Lumeo lenses can be made for it, our support team can help confirm compatibility.

## Why Lumeo Exists

Most smart glasses frames are sold without prescription lenses. Getting prescription lenses fitted for smart glasses has traditionally required finding a specialty lab willing to work with non-standard frames. Lumeo is designed to make this process straightforward and affordable through 39DollarGlasses.

Key considerations for smart glasses lens work include:
- **Optical clarity** — lenses must perform well in the optical zones of the frame
- **Lens fit** — smart glasses frames have specific dimensions and mounting requirements
- **Device compatibility** — lenses must not interfere with frame sensors, cameras, or other components

## Who Is Lumeo For?

Lumeo is a good option for:

- Prescription wearers who own or are planning to purchase compatible smart glasses and want prescription lenses for them
- People who want the functionality of smart glasses without sacrificing their prescription correction

## Lumeo Is Not a Medical Product

Lumeo lenses are optical lenses for smart glasses frames. They are not a medical device and are not intended to treat any condition. Prescription Lumeo lenses are made based on a valid prescription from an eye care professional.
