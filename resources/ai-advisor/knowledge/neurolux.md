# Neurolux Lenses

## What Are Neurolux Lenses?

Neurolux lenses are specialty light-filtering lenses offered by 39DollarGlasses. They are designed for people who are interested in managing their experience with light — particularly those who find certain types of light uncomfortable, distracting, or tiring.

Neurolux lenses use a selective light-filtering approach that targets specific wavelengths associated with high-energy visible light while preserving natural color balance as much as possible.

## Who Might Consider Neurolux?

Neurolux lenses may be worth exploring for people who:

- Find bright artificial lighting uncomfortable or tiring
- Are sensitive to flickering or fluorescent lighting environments
- Spend extended time in environments with strong LED or screen-based lighting
- Are looking for a lens that filters light more selectively than standard tints or standard blue-light coatings

## What Neurolux Is Not

Neurolux lenses are not a medical treatment. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, or cure any medical condition, including migraines, light sensitivity disorders, neurological conditions, or any other health issue.

Individual experiences with light-filtering lenses vary. Neurolux lenses may be helpful for some wearers in managing light comfort, but results are not guaranteed and vary from person to person.

If you experience light sensitivity as part of a medical condition, please consult an eye care professional or your physician before relying on any lens product for symptom management.

## Neurolux and Prescriptions

Neurolux lenses are available with prescription correction. They can be made as single vision or progressive lenses depending on your needs.
